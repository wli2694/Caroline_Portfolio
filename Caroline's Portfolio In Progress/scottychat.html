    ScottyChat

    Mobile website that pairs CMU students with alumni mentors

    Scope: Class Project in Interaction Design Studio with John Zimmerman and Skip Shelly

    Target Audience: Undergraduate Students & Alumni from CMU

    Team: Jayanth Prathipati, Caroline Win

    My Role: UX Designer, Interaction Designer, Web Designer

    Timeline: Sept - Oct 2016

    Project Brief

    uReveal’s data-mining software allows a user to index and search multiple sources including private databases and public newsfeeds and create specialized dictionaries of synonyms. As the software is sold mostly to investigative agencies like NCIS, uReveal asked us to explore additional markets and applications of their technology. Our team focused on academic researchers as our primary user group.

    How might we apply data-mining technology to improve the effectiveness and efficiency of academic researchers in their daily work?

    Understanding the Current Student Experience

    Our first step was to conduct interviews to better understand the values, goals, and pain points of our primary user group: academic researchers.
