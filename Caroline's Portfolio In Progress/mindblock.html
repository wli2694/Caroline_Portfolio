Mindblock

Educational game about emotional health

Scope: Project for Design and Technology for Mobile Experiences Course at USC

My Role: Game Designer & Developer

Timeline: Jan - Aug 2013

This game explores the importance of emotional health and how poor emotional well-being can negatively impact one's physical health. The player chooses different methods of dealing with a frustrating social scenario. Depending on his or her choice, negative emotions such as anger will be released or trapped in the character's mind, leading to a decrease in overall well-being.
