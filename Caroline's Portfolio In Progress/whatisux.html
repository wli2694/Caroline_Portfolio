What is UX Design?

Website that explains UX design to non-designers

Scope: Class Project in Programming Usable Interfaces with Jason Hong, PhD

Target Audience: Friends, Family, & Co-Workers of Designers

My Role: Web Designer & Developer

Timeline: Oct - Dec 2016

Project Brief

For this project, I wanted to create a website that explains UX Design in a simple, interactive, and fun way to people who have no exposure to the term. This site could be sent to relatives, friends, co-workers, bosses, and business people.

How can we bridge the gap between other roles and UX Designers? How can we increase awareness of the value of UX Design?
